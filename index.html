AMCPRAT: AMC Practice tool


Achieve your math competition goals

AMCPRAT lets you select problems of various difficulty from various contests: AMC8, AMC10, AMC12, or AIME. It gives you five of these problems, and gives an easy to use interface for submitting them.

Why use AMCPRAT?

Automate practicing problems

Don't worry about choosing problems you want to do. The system will do it for you. Don't worry about searching for the answer. The system will check if it is right.

Image: Lo√Øc Poivet, CC BY 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons


Easy to use

Simply click a few buttons, and you will get problems from competitions that you want to practice! Submitting problems is also intuitive. Just type in the answer or click the answer choice and press submit.

Image: MJL & House , Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons


Motivate yourself

Enjoy the satisfaction of getting a problem correct. And, since it is so easy to get practice problems, it will be easier to get in the habit of practicing.
